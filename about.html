His Journey

A Story of Creativity
& Dedication

Subhankar Rout is a Product Designer at Capgemini, renowned for crafting scalable, intuitive digital experiences for global enterprises. With over five years in the field, his work spans enterprise SaaS, AI products, and EdTech — impacting millions of users worldwide, and earning recognition for ethical, human-centred design.

Beyond design, Subhankar is a passionate storyteller across mediums. As a bestselling author of three books — The Uncharted Romance, One Year, Many Lessons, and The Goodbye I Never Thought to Say — and an award-winning filmmaker, he merges technical precision with creative narrative to captivate audiences globally.

Additionally, Subhankar has made significant cultural contributions recognized by the Commissioner of Municipality, Rourkela. His leadership of the OdiCoder YouTube channel and management of S4 Cinesphere during college underscore his commitment to sharing knowledge.

UI/UX Design Excellence

Crafting intuitive consumer experiences used by millions globally, with a focus on ethical design.

Film Making Champion

Award-winning filmmaker who has directed and produced multiple short films and music videos.

Published Author × 3

"The Uncharted Romance" (Amazon Kindle bestseller), "One Year, Many Lessons" & "The Goodbye I Never Thought to Say" — three books, one relentless storyteller.

Content Creator

Running two YouTube channels — OdiCoder (tech, local language) and S4 Cinesphere (cinema).
