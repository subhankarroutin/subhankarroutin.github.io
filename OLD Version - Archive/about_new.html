My Journey

A Story of Creativity
& Dedication

Subhankar Rout excels as a Senior UI/UX Designer, renowned for crafting intuitive and impactful consumer experiences worldwide. With over three years in the field, he is awarded and recognized for his commitment to excellence and ethical design practices.

Beyond his professional achievements, Subhankar is a passionate storyteller across various mediums. As a filmmaker, YouTuber, and author of a published English novel, he merges technical expertise with innovative narrative skills, captivating audiences and inspiring creativity.

Additionally, Subhankar has made significant cultural contributions recognized by the Commissioner of Municipality, Rourkela. His leadership of the OdiCoder YouTube channel and management of S4 Cinesphere during college underscore his commitment to sharing knowledge.
