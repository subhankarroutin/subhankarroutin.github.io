About Me

Bridging the gap between user needs and business goals

I'm Subhankar — a Product Designer at Capgemini, working on enterprise-scale digital products for Fortune 500 and global organizations, with a foundation in HCI and design thinking, I obsess over the intersection of clarity, delight and measurable impact.

Previously I've shipped across B2B SaaS platforms, CRM systems, AI-powered tools and consumer apps — always with a focus on reducing complexity and elevating human-computer interaction.

AI-Powered Design

Designing with AI at the frontier

I actively integrate AI tools into my daily design workflow — from rapid ideation to production-ready prototypes. Here's the AI stack that makes me a faster, smarter designer.

🤖
Claude (Anthropic)
AI Design Partner

Used for UX copy refinement, design critique, accessibility audits, and generating design system documentation. Claude helps me think through complex user flows with a research-backed approach.

UX Copy Design Critique A11y Audit
✨
Gemini (Google)
Multimodal Research

Used for competitive research, image analysis of design references, and extracting insights from large design documents. Gemini's multimodal capability helps analyse competitor UI screenshots directly.

Research Image Analysis Multimodal
⚡
ChatGPT (OpenAI)
Prompt Engineering

Certified in Prompt Engineering (Udemy). Use ChatGPT for generating persona narratives, user story drafts, and rapid ideation during discovery phases. Also used for writing structured design briefs and research summaries.

Prompt Eng. Personas User Stories
Figma AI + MCP
AI-Native Design

Using Figma's native AI features and Figma MCP for automating design generation, component suggestions, and connecting Figma directly to AI workflows. This is my primary tool for AI-assisted production design at Capgemini.

Design Gen MCP Workflows Auto-layout AI
🦊
Grok (xAI)
Real-time Insights

Used for real-time trend analysis in UX and design industry. Grok's ability to tap into live conversations on X helps me stay current with emerging design patterns, user pain points, and industry discourse.

Trend Analysis Real-time UX Insights
🎨
Midjourney + DALL·E
Visual Ideation

For rapid concept visualisation, mood boarding, and generating reference images during early design phases. AI image tools let me explore visual directions 10x faster than traditional moodboarding.

Moodboarding Concepts Visual References

AI doesn't replace the designer — it removes the friction between thinking and making.
