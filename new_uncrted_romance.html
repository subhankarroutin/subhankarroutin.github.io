Anshuman is younger. Kavya is older. The world has opinions about both.

Their story begins quietly — with friendship, and the kind of honesty that only genuine closeness carries. Neither of them plans for it to become more. But some things don't wait to be planned.

As their bond deepens, they face the full weight of expectation: families who worry, friends who doubt, and a society that draws its lines with conviction. The question isn't whether they love each other — it's whether that love is enough to outlast everything trying to separate them.

"The Uncharted Romance" is Subhankar Rout's debut novel — warm, honest, and quietly moving. It's a story about what it takes to choose someone when choosing them is hard, and what you discover about yourself when you do.
