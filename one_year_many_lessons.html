What happens when a group of students with zero investment, big dreams, and pure #Jugadu spirit set out to build a B2B startup?

"One Year, Many Lessons" takes readers inside the raw, inspiring, and sometimes chaotic journey of HonourCreators, a digital-service startup started during college days. From forming a team to navigating entrepreneurship challenges, this book captures the thrill of success, the reality of failures, and the tough lessons learned along the way.

All though the journey eventually leads to stepping away from the startup, this story proves that not every exit is a failure. Through one year of real experiences, the author shares insights that help aspiring entrepreneurs avoid common mistakes and understand what building something from scratch truly demands.

In a society where doing something different often earns the label "#Mad", this book embraces that identity with pride — because every meaningful journey begins by breaking the norm.
