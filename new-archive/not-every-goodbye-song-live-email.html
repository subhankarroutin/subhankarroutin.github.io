Why This Is Different

A novel usually begins
on the first page.
This one begins in your ears.

Films have soundtracks. Series have title themes. Novels have always asked readers to imagine the music themselves. The Goodbye I Never Thought to Say changes that — a published work of fiction with its own officially released song, streaming worldwide from today.

By the time the book opens on 24 July, the melody will already be familiar — and the story will feel less like something you are reading, and more like something you remember.

The Novel · 24 July

A love story that doesn’t end where you expect it to.

Varun loves the way patient people do — quietly, without keeping score. Natasha knows exactly what she has in him. Yet one day she falls silent, and the silence stretches into years. When a hospital corridor finally brings them back to the same closed door, they discover the goodbye they never said was never truly an ending.

No villains. No grand gestures. Two people, and the quiet peace of finally understanding.

A Note From the Author

This story has been with me for a long time, and at some point I understood that words alone could not carry all of it. It means a great deal.
